multitapie [Dobry Zbeer](https://www.facebook.com/DobryZbeer/) w
Gliwicach przy
[ul.&nbsp;Zygmunta&nbsp;Starego&nbsp;11a](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Zygmunta+Starego+11a,+44-100+Gliwice/).
